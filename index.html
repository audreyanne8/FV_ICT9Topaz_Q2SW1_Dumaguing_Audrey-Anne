Character Table

CHARACTERS IMAGES ABOUT THEM
Rapunzel Rapunzel is a princess with magical hair, who was trapped in a tower by Mother Gothel. She's a carefree and kind spirit. She's also the lost princess of the Kingdom of Corona.
Flynn Rider Flynn, also known as Eugene Fitzherbert, is the story's deuteragonist (the second most important character in the story). He's known for his charms and made a name for himself as he was a famous thief. However, he changed when he met Rapunzel.
Pascal Pascal is Rapunzel's loyal chameleon pet, and also her good friend.
Mother Gothel Mother Gothel is Rapunzel's supposed 'mother.'However, later on in the story, it was revealed that she kidnapped Rapunzel to use Rapunzel's hair to stay young and beautiful. She is the story's main antagonist.
